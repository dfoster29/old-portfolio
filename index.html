About Me

I currently have the Penguins game playing in the background while I write this bio. I suppose you could say I'm a bit of a hockey fan. I've played hockey most of my life. From street hockey in the driveway, to ice hockey on the frozen pond behind my house. I don't limit myself to just hockey these days though. I've become an avid rock climber. I have a membership to the local climbing gym, which I try to make trips to a few times a week. I'm big in to mountain biking as well as kayaking. I love spending as much time as possible outdoors. I love working with my hands and building things. I've got a 1974 BMW 2002tii which I'm currently in the process of restoring (well sort of). I've had a passion for cars as far back as I can remember. I'm currently driving a 2015 VW GTI which I've done some work too. I absolutely love it. There isn't much better than cruising down back roads with the windows down while my favorite music plays.

I'll have to add more to this bio as it comes to me...
